Our History

TechCon began over a decade ago as a small gathering of software engineers and has since grown into a global conference that attracts thousands of attendees from around the world. It has consistently served as a platform for learning, networking, and innovation across all areas of technology.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 aims to empower developers, engineers, and tech leaders by providing a space where ideas thrive, skills grow, and new innovations are born. Our mission is to democratize access to cutting-edge knowledge and foster inclusive growth in the tech community.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Amina Zahra

AI researcher and ethics advocate, Dr. Zahra is known for her work on fairness in machine learning systems. She delivered a keynote in 2021 on building responsible AI.

John Mathews

Founder of DevOpsNow, John shared his journey building scalable CI/CD pipelines at TechCon 2022, inspiring thousands of engineers globally.

Fatima El Idrissi

A pioneer in cybersecurity from Morocco, Fatima led a 2023 panel on data sovereignty and digital privacy in emerging markets.
